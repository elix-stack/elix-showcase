About Me


I am a graduate of English Education, and I completed my studies on September 14, 2024. For my final project, commonly known as a thesis, I developed an English learning curriculum designed specifically for university students at my campus. The curriculum was developed using the Research and Development method with the Taba Model.


Since graduating, I have been learning web programming independently to build a web-based English learning platform. The goal of this platform is to provide a free and flexible learning resource that can be easily accessed. The target users of my website are not only students but also educators.


In the future, I plan to develop a platform similar to Quizizz. The purpose is to provide educators with free teaching tools equipped with customizable features. This project will take time to complete, considering that I am still a beginner in programming and currently learning on my own. Moreover, it will also require funding, as a platform like Quizizz needs a large database. I hope to stay healthy and find a job soon so that I can bring this project to life.
